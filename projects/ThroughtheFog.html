Through the Fog is a third-person roguelite exploration role-playing-game where you explore a mysterious abandoned city plagued by a disorienting fog and buildings that change each night. Your goal is to get people back into the city and find out what exactly went wrong. So, you go into the fog each night after the buildings have moved, to map out the city, then sell the populous maps based on the new positions. Along the way, you find secrets and help NPCs with quests to uncover the past, and the nature of the curse that fell upon the once-thriving metropolis.

As the narrative lead, I created the story of the world and worked with the team to ensure narrative consistency throughout gameplay and the world the player interacts with. I wrote all dialogue, the quest descriptions, and wrote scripts and directed the voice acting for the in-game audio logs. I also designed the tutorials and quest steps for the game. As the co-producer, I worked with the other producer on our documentation requirements for the course, creating and supervising the progress of development milestones, and creating production plans.
