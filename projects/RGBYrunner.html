When I first heard of the College of Spirits subclass, I was very intrigued, but found some of the written-in flavor to be missing. One of the suggested spellfoci was a Tarokka deck, which I felt had the most flavor for the spirit of the subclass. However, the only Tarokka deck from WotC did not contain a card for any of the “tales” for the class. So, I made a deck of my own, and since printing high quality cards is not something I have the equipment for, I made an application to use it digitally.

The application has 5 buttons at the top, a d6 and d8 on the left, a d10 and d12 on the right, and a shuffle button in the middle. The dice buttons are toggles, limiting the roll to the toggled dice. You can use your mouse, or hand if you have a touchscreen, to swipe the card over to the other side.
